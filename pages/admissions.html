Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be simple, transparent, and parent-friendly. We welcome students who are eager to learn and grow in a nurturing environment.

Step 1: Enquiry

Parents may contact the school or submit an enquiry form to receive admission details.

Step 2: Application

Complete the application form along with required documents for initial evaluation.

Step 3: Interaction

An interaction or assessment may be conducted depending on the grade applied for.

Step 4: Confirmation

Upon successful completion, admission confirmation will be provided by the school.
